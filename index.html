About Melon Day

Melon Day is a national holiday of Turkmenistan celebrating the glorious Turkmen musk melon. MelonDaySF is a non-profit organization dedicated to sharing the joy and fruit of this tasty holiday in San Francisco. Join us on August 5th, 2023 for the 7th annual celebration of Melon Day in Dolores Park.
