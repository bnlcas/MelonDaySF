Melon Day

Melon Day is a national holiday of Turkmenistan celebrating the glorious Turkmen musk melon.

It falls on the second Sunday of August and is the cause for much celebration.

MelonDaySF is a non-profit organization dedicated to sharing the joy (and fruit) of this tasty holiday in San Francisco.

Join us on August 8th, 2021 for our 5th annual celebration of Melons in Dolores Park.
